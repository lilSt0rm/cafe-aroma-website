About Us

Café Aroma is not just a coffee shop – it’s a little world of warmth, comfort, and creativity. The idea behind this project was to design a cozy digital space where coffee lovers can feel the same energy they get when stepping into their favorite café.

This website was created by Lily_dev (LilStorm) as a web development project. My goal was to bring together trendy design, smooth animations, and a welcoming café atmosphere into a multi-page website.

The project represents my passion for web development, design, and storytelling. Just like a good cup of coffee, coding is about balance – the right mix of creativity, functionality, and a touch of art.

✨ The Idea

The concept is inspired by the cozy feeling of sitting in a café with friends, sipping coffee, and enjoying small moments of joy. I wanted to recreate that vibe online through a modern website full of warmth and animation.
